# 日常通用口语模板

> 这一篇优先解决“最常用、最容易开口”的日常表达。  
> 先背熟这些，再去扩展更复杂的场景。

---

## 1. 打招呼（10 句）

1. `Hi, how are you?`
2. `How's it going?`
3. `Good morning.`
4. `Good afternoon.`
5. `Good evening.`
6. `Nice to see you.`
7. `Long time no see.`
8. `How have you been?`
9. `It's good to see you again.`
10. `Hope you're doing well.`

## 2. 自我表达近况（8 句）

11. `I'm doing well, thanks.`
12. `I've been pretty busy lately.`
13. `Things are going well.`
14. `I'm a little tired today.`
15. `I've had a long day.`
16. `Everything is fine so far.`
17. `I'm just taking it easy today.`
18. `I've been learning a lot recently.`

## 3. 表达感谢（8 句）

19. `Thank you so much.`
20. `Thanks a lot.`
21. `I really appreciate it.`
22. `That means a lot to me.`
23. `Thanks for your help.`
24. `Thanks for your time.`
25. `I appreciate your support.`
26. `I'm grateful for that.`

## 4. 表达抱歉（8 句）

27. `I'm sorry.`
28. `I'm really sorry about that.`
29. `Sorry for the trouble.`
30. `Sorry I'm late.`
31. `Sorry to keep you waiting.`
32. `I didn't mean to do that.`
33. `It was my mistake.`
34. `Please accept my apology.`

## 5. 请求帮助（10 句）

35. `Could you help me?`
36. `Could you help me with this?`
37. `Can you show me how to do this?`
38. `Would you mind helping me for a minute?`
39. `Can I ask you something?`
40. `Could you explain that again?`
41. `Can you give me an example?`
42. `Could you slow down a little?`
43. `Can you speak a bit more clearly?`
44. `Could you point me in the right direction?`

## 6. 表达听不懂（8 句）

45. `I don't understand.`
46. `I didn't catch that.`
47. `Could you say that again?`
48. `Could you repeat that, please?`
49. `What do you mean by that?`
50. `I'm not sure I follow.`
51. `Could you explain it in a simpler way?`
52. `Let me make sure I understand.`

## 7. 表达喜欢 / 不喜欢（8 句）

53. `I like it.`
54. `I love it.`
55. `I'm really into that.`
56. `That sounds great.`
57. `I'm not a big fan of it.`
58. `It's not really my thing.`
59. `I don't like it that much.`
60. `I'd rather do something else.`

## 8. 结束对话（10 句）

61. `I have to go now.`
62. `Talk to you later.`
63. `See you soon.`
64. `See you next time.`
65. `Take care.`
66. `Have a nice day.`
67. `Have a good one.`
68. `It was nice talking to you.`
69. `Let's catch up again soon.`
70. `Bye for now.`

---

## 使用建议

- 先优先背 `35-52` 这组，它们最实用
- 每次挑 10 句，先读熟再替换关键词
- 把这些句子录音，反复听自己的发音
