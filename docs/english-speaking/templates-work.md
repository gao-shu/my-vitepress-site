# 工作沟通口语模板

> 这一篇适合工作、协作、汇报、催进度、说明问题等常见表达。  
> 重点不是高级词汇，而是**简单、清楚、礼貌**。

---

## 1. 开始沟通（8 句）

1. `I'd like to discuss something with you.`
2. `Do you have a minute?`
3. `Can we talk about this for a moment?`
4. `I want to give you a quick update.`
5. `I need your input on this.`
6. `I'd like to get your opinion.`
7. `Let's go over this together.`
8. `I want to make sure we're aligned.`

## 2. 反馈进度（10 句）

9. `I'm working on it now.`
10. `I've already started on it.`
11. `It's currently in progress.`
12. `I'm almost done.`
13. `I've completed the first part.`
14. `I'm waiting for the final confirmation.`
15. `We are on schedule.`
16. `We're making good progress.`
17. `There are still a few things to finish.`
18. `I expect to finish it by tomorrow.`

## 3. 说明问题（10 句）

19. `We're facing a small issue here.`
20. `There seems to be a problem.`
21. `I found a bug in the system.`
22. `The result is not what we expected.`
23. `Something is blocking the progress.`
24. `We're missing some information.`
25. `The data doesn't look correct.`
26. `This part needs to be checked again.`
27. `There may be a risk here.`
28. `We need to fix this before moving on.`

## 4. 请求支持（10 句）

29. `Could you take a look at this?`
30. `Can you help me review this part?`
31. `I need your support on this issue.`
32. `Could you confirm this for me?`
33. `Would you mind checking it again?`
34. `Can we get some help from the team?`
35. `Could you share your thoughts on this?`
36. `I need more details before I continue.`
37. `Please let me know what you think.`
38. `I'd appreciate your feedback.`

## 5. 对齐信息（10 句）

39. `Just to make sure we're on the same page.`
40. `Let me summarize the current situation.`
41. `Here's my understanding so far.`
42. `Please correct me if I'm wrong.`
43. `So the next step is clear.`
44. `Let's confirm the priority first.`
45. `We should align on the timeline.`
46. `Let's agree on the scope.`
47. `We need a clear owner for this task.`
48. `Let's define the next action item.`

## 6. 礼貌催办（10 句）

49. `Just following up on this.`
50. `Do you have any updates on this?`
51. `Could you let me know the current status?`
52. `I wanted to check if there's any progress.`
53. `When do you think this will be ready?`
54. `Could you send it to me once it's done?`
55. `Please keep me posted.`
56. `This is a gentle reminder.`
57. `It would be great if we could move this forward today.`
58. `Please let me know if anything is blocking you.`

## 7. 表达建议（10 句）

59. `I have a suggestion.`
60. `Maybe we can try another approach.`
61. `It might be better to do it this way.`
62. `What if we simplify this part?`
63. `I think we should focus on the key issue first.`
64. `We may want to review this again.`
65. `It would help if we clarified the requirement first.`
66. `I recommend starting with the smaller task.`
67. `Let's keep the solution simple for now.`
68. `We can improve it in the next iteration.`

## 8. 收尾表达（12 句）

69. `Thanks for the update.`
70. `That sounds good to me.`
71. `Let's move forward with this plan.`
72. `I'll take care of the next step.`
73. `Please let me know if anything changes.`
74. `I'll keep you updated.`
75. `Let's stay in touch on this.`
76. `Thanks for your support.`
77. `I think we're in good shape now.`
78. `This should work for now.`
79. `Let's review it again tomorrow.`
80. `We can close this for now.`

---

## 使用建议

- 工作口语里，`简洁` 比 `高级` 更重要
- 重点记 `19-38` 和 `49-58`
- 把你自己常见的工作场景套进去练
