# 电话 / 视频沟通模板

> 这一篇适合打电话、语音、视频会议时使用。  
> 这类场景最常见的问题是：**听不清、卡顿、确认信息、礼貌衔接**。

---

## 1. 接通与确认（10 句）

1. `Hello, this is [your name].`
2. `Hi, am I speaking to [name]?`
3. `Can you hear me clearly?`
4. `Can you see my screen?`
5. `Is the audio okay on your side?`
6. `Thanks for joining the call.`
7. `Let's wait a minute for the others to join.`
8. `Can we start now?`
9. `I'm calling about today's update.`
10. `I'm reaching out to discuss this issue.`

## 2. 听不清 / 网络问题（10 句）

11. `Your voice is breaking up.`
12. `The connection is a bit unstable.`
13. `I can't hear you very well.`
14. `Could you say that again?`
15. `Could you speak a little louder?`
16. `I think your microphone is muted.`
17. `The screen is frozen on my side.`
18. `You cut out for a second.`
19. `Let me reconnect quickly.`
20. `Could you type it in the chat?`

## 3. 转入正题（10 句）

21. `Let's get to the main point.`
22. `I'm calling to follow up on this matter.`
23. `I want to quickly go over the current status.`
24. `There are two things I'd like to discuss.`
25. `The main reason for this call is this.`
26. `Let's review the issue step by step.`
27. `I'll keep it brief.`
28. `Here's what we need to decide today.`
29. `Let's focus on the key issue first.`
30. `I want to make sure we're aligned before moving on.`

## 4. 电话中确认信息（10 句）

31. `Let me repeat that to make sure I got it right.`
32. `So you're saying that...?`
33. `Just to confirm, the deadline is Friday, right?`
34. `Can you send me the details later?`
35. `I'll write that down.`
36. `Let me check if I understood correctly.`
37. `Could you confirm the final version?`
38. `Who will handle this part?`
39. `What should I do next?`
40. `I'll follow up after the call.`

## 5. 留言 / 回拨 / 收尾（10 句）

41. `He's not available right now.`
42. `Can I take a message?`
43. `Could you ask him to call me back?`
44. `I'll call you back later.`
45. `Let's continue this later.`
46. `I'll send a summary after this call.`
47. `Thanks for your time.`
48. `Talk to you again soon.`
49. `Have a great day.`
50. `Goodbye for now.`

---

## 使用建议

- 电话 / 视频里最关键的是 `3-5`、`11-20`、`31-40`
- 如果紧张，就反复练“确认型句子”
- 真实通话时，宁可慢一点，也不要一口气说太长
