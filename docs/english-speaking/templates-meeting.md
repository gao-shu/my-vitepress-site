# 会议表达口语模板

> 这一篇适合开会时发言、补充、提问、同意、不同意、总结。  
> 会上最重要的是：**短句、清晰、自然接话**。

---

## 1. 会议开场（10 句）

1. `Let's get started.`
2. `Thanks, everyone, for joining.`
3. `Let's begin with a quick overview.`
4. `The purpose of today's meeting is to discuss this issue.`
5. `I'll briefly walk through the current status.`
6. `Let's start with the main point.`
7. `We have a few things to cover today.`
8. `First, let's review where we are.`
9. `I'd like to start with a short update.`
10. `Let's go through the agenda together.`

## 2. 补充观点（10 句）

11. `I'd like to add something here.`
12. `Let me add one more point.`
13. `I want to build on that.`
14. `From my perspective, this is important.`
15. `I think there's another angle to consider.`
16. `One thing we should keep in mind is this.`
17. `I'd also like to point out that...`
18. `Another thing to consider is the timeline.`
19. `We should also think about the impact.`
20. `There's one more detail I'd like to mention.`

## 3. 提问与确认（10 句）

21. `Could you clarify that?`
22. `Can you explain that in more detail?`
23. `What exactly do you mean by that?`
24. `Do I understand this correctly?`
25. `Just to confirm, are we saying that...?`
26. `Could you walk us through that part again?`
27. `What is the expected outcome here?`
28. `Who will be responsible for this?`
29. `When do we need this to be done?`
30. `What are the next steps?`

## 4. 表达同意（10 句）

31. `I agree with that.`
32. `That makes sense.`
33. `I think that's a good idea.`
34. `I'm on the same page.`
35. `That sounds reasonable.`
36. `I support that approach.`
37. `I think we're aligned on this.`
38. `That's exactly what I was thinking.`
39. `I completely agree.`
40. `That works for me.`

## 5. 表达不同意或保留意见（10 句）

41. `I see your point, but I have a concern.`
42. `I'm not sure that's the best option.`
43. `I think we may need to look at this differently.`
44. `I understand, but I don't fully agree.`
45. `There might be a better way to handle this.`
46. `I'm a little concerned about the risk.`
47. `I think we should be careful here.`
48. `I'd prefer a simpler solution.`
49. `We may want to revisit this decision.`
50. `I'm not convinced this will solve the issue.`

## 6. 打断与接回话题（10 句）

51. `Sorry to interrupt, but...`
52. `Can I jump in for a second?`
53. `Before we move on, I'd like to mention something.`
54. `Let me quickly add one thing.`
55. `Can we go back to the previous point?`
56. `I'd like to return to the main issue.`
57. `Let's stay focused on the key problem.`
58. `We seem to be getting off track.`
59. `Can we get back to the original question?`
60. `Let's bring the discussion back to the goal.`

## 7. 总结与收尾（10 句）

61. `Let me summarize what we've discussed.`
62. `So here's the key takeaway.`
63. `We've agreed on the next step.`
64. `The action items are clear now.`
65. `I'll share the summary after the meeting.`
66. `Let's follow up on this tomorrow.`
67. `Thanks for the discussion.`
68. `I think we made good progress today.`
69. `Let's wrap it up here.`
70. `Thanks, everyone.`

---

## 使用建议

- 会议表达不需要长句，短句更稳
- `21-30`、`31-40`、`61-70` 最值得优先背
- 开会前先准备 3 句你一定会用到的话
