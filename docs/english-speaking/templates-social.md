# 社交寒暄口语模板

> 这一篇适合初次见面、轻松交流、聊天破冰、发出邀请、礼貌回应。  
> 社交口语的重点是：**自然、轻松、不要太书面**。

---

## 1. 初次见面（10 句）

1. `Nice to meet you.`
2. `It's great to meet you.`
3. `I've heard a lot about you.`
4. `Thanks for taking the time to meet me.`
5. `I'm glad we finally met.`
6. `Please call me [name].`
7. `Let me introduce myself.`
8. `It's a pleasure to meet you.`
9. `I've been looking forward to meeting you.`
10. `Thanks for being here.`

## 2. 轻松聊天（10 句）

11. `How's your day going?`
12. `What have you been up to lately?`
13. `How was your weekend?`
14. `Did you do anything fun recently?`
15. `That's interesting. Tell me more.`
16. `That sounds really nice.`
17. `I've had a similar experience.`
18. `I know what you mean.`
19. `That must have been exciting.`
20. `You seem really passionate about that.`

## 3. 聊工作 / 学习 / 兴趣（10 句）

21. `What do you do?`
22. `What are you working on these days?`
23. `What are you studying right now?`
24. `How did you get into that field?`
25. `What do you usually do in your free time?`
26. `What kind of music do you like?`
27. `Do you have any hobbies?`
28. `I've been learning English recently.`
29. `I'm trying to improve my speaking skills.`
30. `I'm interested in technology and self-learning.`

## 4. 发出邀请（10 句）

31. `Would you like to join us?`
32. `Do you want to grab a coffee sometime?`
33. `Let's have lunch together sometime.`
34. `Would you be free this weekend?`
35. `How about meeting up next week?`
36. `Do you want to hang out after work?`
37. `It would be great to catch up sometime.`
38. `Let me know if you'd like to come.`
39. `You're welcome to join us.`
40. `I'd love to see you there.`

## 5. 礼貌拒绝与回应（10 句）

41. `I'd love to, but I already have plans.`
42. `That sounds great, but I may not be available.`
43. `Maybe next time.`
44. `Thanks for the invitation.`
45. `I really appreciate the invite.`
46. `I'll let you know soon.`
47. `I'm not sure yet, but I'll check my schedule.`
48. `That sounds like fun.`
49. `Let's plan for another time.`
50. `I hope we can do that soon.`

---

## 使用建议

- 社交口语不要追求复杂，轻松最重要
- `11-20` 和 `31-40` 非常实用
- 你可以把 `28-30` 作为自己的固定介绍句
